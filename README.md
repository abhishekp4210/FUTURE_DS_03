📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard
Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes marketing funnel performance to identify conversion trends, channel effectiveness, ROI, and customer acquisition efficiency. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and actionable insights to support data-driven marketing decisions.

Objectives
Analyze marketing funnel stages from impressions to conversions.
Evaluate channel performance using clicks, ROI, and acquisition cost.
Identify high-performing and low-performing marketing channels.
Monitor conversion rates and customer engagement.
Generate business insights and recommendations.

Business Insights & Recommendations
Key Insights
Email campaigns achieved the highest ROI.
Social media channels generated the highest impressions.
February recorded the lowest click performance.
Higher acquisition costs did not always result in higher ROI.
Recommendations
Increase investment in high-performing channels.
Optimize campaigns during low-performing periods.
Focus on high-converting campaign types.
Continuously monitor ROI and acquisition cost.
